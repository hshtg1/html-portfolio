My Top 5 Favourite Movies of All-Time

Pulp Fiction

A really well-written movie. The story encompasses a life of mafia in a funny take on it. It's also an unexpected love story. It's just so satisfying to watch with many clever lines and an overlook on a thug life.

Ghost Story

A beautiful movie that made me shed tears. Not many movies can do that. An artistic vision of grief and loss.

Scream

This brutal horror criminal movie is filled with unexpected plot twists. It's just so satisfying to watch. Scream is filled with horror movie references.

Psycho

One of the first horror movies that came to be. Made by the legendary Alfred Hitchcok, the story has a crazy major plot twist and many interesting quotes. Psycho is scary in it's own very original way. Many movies like Scream are modeled on it.

Shrek

Probably one of the best animations of it's kind. Shrek is a movie for everyone!
